# Text Messaging (SMS) Terms & Disclosures

## How to Text BounceBack Data Recovery

To start a text conversation with us, text **“START”** (or your question) to:

**(636) 333-0030**

You may also text this number after contacting us by phone or through our website if you request text updates regarding your service.

## Types of Messages You May Receive

By texting **BounceBack Data Recovery LLC**, you agree to receive conversational SMS messages related to customer care and services, which may include:

- Appointment scheduling and coordination (drop-off / pickup / shipping)
- Service, repair, or recovery status updates
- Estimates, invoices, and payment-related notifications
- Responses to your questions and support requests

## Message Frequency

Message frequency may vary depending on your conversation and service needs.

## Message & Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan.

## How to Opt Out

You may opt out of receiving SMS messages at any time by replying:

**STOP**

After you reply STOP, you may receive one final confirmation message confirming your opt-out request.

## How to Get Help

For assistance, reply:

**HELP**

Or contact us directly:

**Phone:** (636) 333-0030
**Email:** support@bouncebackdata.com
