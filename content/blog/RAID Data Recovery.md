BounceBack’s St Louis cleanroom lab just recovered a very complicated Inforstor iSCSI RAID. It was two separate RAID configurations which were then arrayed together. Essentially a “nested” RAID within a RAID. A riddle wrapped in a mystery wrapped in an enigma. We achieved a full recovery for a business that owns many businesses, the critical data for all of them was on this RAID-in-a-RAID.

We think they should get into the riddle business, or maybe 10,000 piece jigsaw puzzles of a white sky with clouds.
